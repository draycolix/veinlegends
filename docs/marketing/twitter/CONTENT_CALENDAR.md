## Crisis Management Plan

**If token dumps:**
- Transparent: "Here's what happened, here's what we're doing"
- No panic, no blame
- Show long-term plan (vesting, buybacks)

**If game breaks:**
- Acknowledge within 1 hour
- Fix ETA
- Reward reporters

**If FUD:**
- Address with data, not emotion
- Link to docs/whitepaper
- Don't engage trolls
